# Vintage Restaurant Sales Analysis & Forecast

A Power BI dashboard analyzing 3 years of historical sales data for a seafood restaurant, identifying seasonal patterns and forecasting sales for the year ahead.

## Overview

- **Data**: 36 months (3 years) of historical sales figures
- **Forecasting method**: Two regression models built and compared in Excel; the stronger regression equation was used to project monthly sales for Year 4 (12 months ahead)
- **Deliverable**: An interactive Power BI dashboard (`.pbix`) communicating trends, seasonality, and forecasts

## Dashboard Pages

- **Home** — landing page introducing the dashboard
- **Line Chart** — actual sales vs. forecast sales over time, with KPI cards and a year-over-year clustered column comparison
- **Seasonal Pattern Chart** — average sales per month across the 3-year period, highlighting recurring seasonal peaks and troughs
- **Forecasted Sales Chart** — Year 4 forecast visualization with a gauge and clustered/line chart combination

## Tech Stack

- Microsoft Excel (regression analysis for forecasting)
- Power BI (interactive dashboard, DAX measures, data modeling)

## Getting Started

Open `Vintage_Restaurant_Sales_Analysis___Forecast.pbix` in [Power BI Desktop](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/desktop/) (free) to explore the interactive report.
